Refund Policy

1. Free Plan

The Free plan requires no payment and can be used indefinitely with 10 leads per day.

2. Paid Subscriptions

Pro ($29/month) and Agency ($99/month) plans are billed monthly via Paddle. All payments are processed securely by Paddle.

3. Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you retain Pro/Agency features until then.

4. Refunds

We offer refunds on a case-by-case basis within 7 days of the initial payment. Contact support@leadgen.ai with your account email and reason for the refund request.

5. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback through your payment provider, we reserve the right to suspend your account pending resolution.
